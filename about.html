DEFAB

ABOUT

Create a Website for showing information about accessible buildings,public places,collages etc around a locality. By using the website the user can easily understand whether the building is accessible or not and also can locate nearest accessible place by help of maps. The impact of disability is radically changed on the website because the website removes barriers to communication and interaction that many people face in the physical world.
